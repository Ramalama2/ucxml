Information collected when creating/editing a contact...

  • Title - Text you would like to be displayed along side the last, first name of the contact
  • Member Of - Contact Holder that contact is contained in.
  • First Name - The first name is the 2nd part of the contact label on the phone
  • Last Name - The last name is the first part of the contact label on the phone
  • Nickname - The nickname the contact belongs to. This information will be displayed along side the title, when viewing the contact using the phone interface.
  • Phone Numbers - Each contact holds 4 phone numbers.
    • Office Phone
    • Home Phone
    • Cell Phone
    • Other Phone
